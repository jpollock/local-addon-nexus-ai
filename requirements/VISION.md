## Why This Matters

### The Problem
Managing multiple WordPress sites is tedious:
- "Which sites have outdated plugins?" → SSH into 20 sites
- "What's the PHP version distribution?" → Manual spreadsheet
- "Install security plugin on all sites" → Click through 20 admin UIs
- "Which sites need attention?" → No idea until something breaks

### The Solution
**Nexus AI** makes your fleet intelligent:
- **Ask** → "Sites with outdated PHP" → See them instantly
- **Discover** → Saved queries show what needs work
- **Automate** → "Update all sites" → Watch it happen
- **Prevent** → Anomaly detection catches issues early
